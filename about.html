About

I'm a Data Science leader with 13+ years of experience delivering measurable business impact through decision science, advanced analytics, and AI/ML solutions. I started The Decision Signal as a platform to share practical, no-fluff insights for data science practitioners and leaders who want to cut through the noise.

Currently at Tesco Bengaluru, I lead AI-driven transformation of internal audit and risk — deploying anomaly detection at scale, building LLM-powered knowledge tools, and helping senior leadership make faster, smarter decisions with data they can trust.

Before Tesco, I've built models and led analytics across retail pricing, supply chain optimisation, marketing mix modelling, and customer analytics — for global FMCG clients, CPG retailers, and financial services firms.

My work sits at the intersection of rigorous statistics, ML engineering, and executive storytelling. I believe data science's real value isn't in the models — it's in the decisions they enable. That's what I write about, teach, and coach.
